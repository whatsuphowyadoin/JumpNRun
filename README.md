# JumpNRun
 Welcome to an infinite version of jump n run. Play the Game with Hades, the ancient Greek god of the underworld, trough three levels: the underworld level, the water level and the heaven. Gather the coins you find on the way to the gate that leads to the next level.Â  Be careful! There are poisonous bats that can kill you. The green monster won't do anything, still be on guard, you can fall between the blocks and also lose. After you found the third gate, after passing it, you will be sent to the first level. This way, you can play the game as long as you wish. The goal of the game is to gather as many coins as you can. Have plan playing!
